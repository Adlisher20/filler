Welcome to FillerLoad

Filler load platform will help you to find extra load used to utilize the vacant/ unutilized space in a vehicle carrying consignments. The platform also enables you to share any additional load to more than 1000 vendors in your region.

What drives us?

Creating New Possibilities

The growth of tech in the past decade has enabled us to think big and explore new possibilities. Filler Load aims to take advantage of the cutting-edge technology in the area of programming machine learning and artificial intelligence.

 Supporting Small Businesses

Due to increase profit margins, vendors will be able to provide best in industry rate to the small business who wants to move their material. This will be a big support to entrepreneurs and budding businesses.

Committed for a better future

Burning of fossil fuel is one of the key reason for global warming. The rate of fuel consumption is increasing year on year. Complete utilization of space in a vehicle will help in reducing the carbon footprint of the logistics and transportation industry.
